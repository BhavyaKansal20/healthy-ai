Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026  |  Your privacy is our responsibility

What Data We Collect

Full name, email address and hashed password for your account. Health parameters you enter in prediction forms — age, BMI, blood pressure, glucose levels etc. Prediction results and generated PDF reports. Basic session information to keep you logged in.

How We Use Your Data

Your health parameters are used as input to the ML prediction model. To generate personalized PDF health reports. To display your report history in the dashboard. No data is used for marketing, advertising, or sold to any third party.

Data Security

Passwords are secured with SHA-256 hashing. The database is a local SQLite file — no cloud storage involved. Session tokens are secure and time-limited. No sensitive data is stored in plain text.

Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time. You can download all your reports. You have the right to request a data export. You can request correction of any inaccurate data.

Cookies

We use only session cookies to maintain your login state. No tracking, advertising, or third-party cookies are used. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser.

Contact Us

For any privacy concerns, write to us at: healthyai@hackathon2026.in
